# Safarly – AI-Powered Trip Expense Planner

Traveling with friends or family is exciting, but managing expenses during a trip can be challenging. Many travelers set a budget before leaving, yet they often forget to record expenses, lose track of small purchases, or realize only after the trip that they have overspent. This makes it difficult to understand where the money went, stay within budget, or plan future trips more effectively.

**Safarly** is an AI-powered Trip Expense Planner designed to solve these problems. The application allows users to create a trip, set a total budget, and organize expenses into categories such as transportation, accommodation, food, shopping, entertainment, and more. Throughout the journey, users can quickly log every expense through a clean and intuitive interface while monitoring their remaining budget in real time.

Unlike traditional expense trackers, Safarly uses AI to provide meaningful insights instead of simply storing data. By analyzing spending patterns, the AI identifies categories where users are overspending and offers personalized recommendations to help reduce unnecessary expenses. It can suggest smarter spending habits, highlight unusual purchases, and provide budget-saving tips before users exceed their planned budget. These proactive insights help travelers make informed financial decisions while the trip is still in progress.

At the end of each trip, Safarly generates a detailed financial summary with total expenses, category-wise spending, budget comparisons, and visual charts. These reports help users understand their spending habits, identify areas for improvement, and plan future trips with greater confidence.

Safarly transforms travel budgeting into a smarter and more organized experience. By combining real-time expense tracking with AI-powered recommendations, it enables travelers to stay within budget, reduce unnecessary costs, and focus on enjoying their journey instead of worrying about their finances. Whether traveling solo, with friends, or with family, Safarly serves as a reliable companion that makes every trip more financially efficient and stress-free.
